Events

  • Business Seminars and Conferences

    Seminars and conferences featuring guest speakers and experts on topics of interest to the local business community, such as entrepreneurship, finance, and sustainable business practices.

  • Business Networking Breakfast

    Breakfast event where local business owners and professionals can gather to network, exchange ideas, and discuss potential collaborations.

  • Business Awards Ceremony

    Recognize outstanding businesses and entrepreneurs in Huancayo with an annual awards ceremony. Categories include "Best Small Business," "Innovation Award," and "Community Impact Award."

  • Trade Fairs and Expos

    Host trade fairs or expos showcasing local businesses and their products/services. This provides an opportunity for businesses to promote themselves to a wider audience and potentially attract new customers or partners.
