Membership level

NP Membership (Non-Profit Membership)

Description

Designed specifically for non-profit organizations.

Fee

No fee required.

Benefits

Basic membership benefits tailored to the needs and interests of non-profit organizations, such as access to resources, networking opportunities, and possibly discounted or free attendance to certain events.

Bronze Membership

Description

Entry-level membership tier.

Fee

Moderate or nominal fee.

Benefits

Basic benefits package, which may include access to resources, limited networking opportunities, and possibly discounts on select services or events.

Silver Membership

Description

Mid-tier membership level.

Fee

Higher than Bronze, but still affordable.

Benefits

Enhanced benefits compared to Bronze, such as increased access to resources, expanded networking opportunities, and additional perks like discounted rates for events or services.

Gold Membership

Description

Premium membership level.

Fee

Highest level of membership fee.

Benefits

This package include exclusive access to specialized resources, priority networking opportunities, premium advertising options on our website, substantial discounts on events or services, and personalized training sessions or priority customer service.
